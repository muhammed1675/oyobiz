Oyo Biz

Oyo Biz is a free online business directory for Oyo State, Nigeria. It helps people search for and discover local businesses — restaurants, shops, services, and more — across Ibadan, Ogbomoso, Oyo, and other cities in the state. Business owners can create a free listing so customers can find them, view their contact details, and leave reviews.

How we use your Google account

If you choose to sign in with Google, we use your name and email address only to create and secure your Oyo Biz account, so you can list a business, save favorite listings, and leave reviews. We do not access your Google contacts, files, or any other Google data, and we never post anything on your behalf.
